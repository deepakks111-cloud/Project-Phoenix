## Purpose

This document records all significant architectural, engineering, and project decisions made during the development of Project Phoenix.

Once recorded, decisions remain effective unless superseded by a later documented decision.

# Decision History

---

## PHX-DEC-001

**Date:** 2026-06-23

### Decision

GitHub shall serve as the single source of truth for Project Phoenix.

### Reason

- Version control
- Traceability
- Reliable backups
- Professional development workflow

**Status**

Approved

---

## PHX-DEC-002

**Date:** 2026-06-23

### Decision

Project development shall follow the Development Protocol v1.0.

### Reason

Ensure disciplined engineering practices throughout the project lifecycle.

**Status**

Approved

---

## PHX-DEC-003

**Date:** 2026-06-23

### Decision

Only one engineering artifact will be generated, reviewed, uploaded, and committed at a time.

Development does not proceed until the previous artifact is confirmed committed.

### Reason

- Prevent skipped artifacts
- Keep repository synchronized
- Maintain implementation quality
- Simplify project tracking

**Status**

Approved

---

## PHX-DEC-004

**Date:** 2026-06-23

### Decision

Overall project progress shall increase only after a completed and committed engineering artifact.

### Reason

Progress must reflect implemented work rather than planning or discussion.

**Status**

Approved
